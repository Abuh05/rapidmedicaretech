Think Hard And Focus On The Patient's Well-Being

RapidMedicare is an online marketplace that allows licensed doctors and pharmacists create customized clinics for video or voice consultation to enhance swift and seamless patient diagnosis and treatment

All over the world, 2.9 million people die yearly from lack of access to healthcare coupled with the high cost of healthcare delivery. Our aim is to bridge that gap by facilitating swift access to healthcare and near instantaneously matching you with doctors or pharmacists for tele-appointment bookings/consultation

More so, patient healthcare records are decentralized and porous to attackers. At RapidMedicare, we centralize patient health records and encrypt them end-to-end. Details from patient consultations with the doctor, drug prescriptions, medical test bookings, drug orders, etc, are all end-to-end encrypted, and these are the details that will become the centralized electronic health record for reference in subsequent consultation(s)

About Us

At RapidMedicare, we like to say that we’re the first choice of healthcare systems who are ready to make a change.
